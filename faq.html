What's Bigfoots diet?

Since he is a mammal, he's believed to follow an omnivorous diet, eating a range of mostly plants as well as fish, elk, deer, and small rodents.

How tall is Bigfoot?

He's been described to be 6-19 feet tall.

Are there more than one Bigfoot?

Yes, there are believed to be at least 2,000 Sasquatches in the world. Consisting of both young and old Bigfoots. Families of them potentially roam the forests.

What do scientists say about Bigfoot?

Most scientists are skeptical in Bigfoots existence due to the lack of evidence. No bones, hair samples, or DNA have proved him to be real. There are also no clear photos or videos that can identify Bigfoots actuality. Many researchers argue that if a population of large primates existed, we would have found more concrete proof by now.

Has the FBI investigated Bigfoot?

Yes. The FBI conducted a DNA test on what was initially believed to be Bigfoots hair, was found to be deer hair.

What would happen if Bigfoot was proven real?

If Bigfoot were proven to exist with undeniable evidence of fossils, a body, or clear camera footage, this would open the door to new possibilities. Evidence of Bigfoots existence would transition his place in folklore to scientific fact. Tourism might increase in places associated with Bigfoot along with merchandise sales and more people would search for Bigfoot. If people saw that he was real, they would be more willing to believe in other cryptids and previously unimaginable possibilities.

How has He impacted our Culture?

Bigfoot has impacted our culture in all ways you can imagine. Inspiring famous films such as Harry and the Hendersons, along with many horror movies, documentaries centered around finding Bigfoot, and the children's movie Bigfoot Family on netflix. There are many other films and shows about, but this is just to list a few.

Bigfoot has been so influential to our lives and has grown to be part of our culture. He's featured in advertisements, on merchandise, a mascot for sports teams, and is an icon representing forests & national parks. Further, there have been many books and novels written about Bigfoot and his mysterious nature such as The Secret History of Bigfoot, written by John O'Connor. To further add on to his impressive resume, he has museums created in his name. He is one of the most recognizable beings of our century!

My Experience

When I was younger, my cousins and I spent a lot of time playing in the woods behind her house. One day, we found a strange structure made out of sticks and branches. We've never built anything out there and there were no one other people nearby. There was also random trash around it, which was weird for the middle of the woods. We looked around and noticed big and abnormal footprints on the ground, too big to be either of ours. After that, the whole area felt off, as if we were being watched. To this day I've wondered what that was and if it was Bigfoots doing.
