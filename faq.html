Frequently Asked Bigfoot Questions

  1. Is He real?

    2. This is a tricky question because although there have been so many reported sightings of him, no evidence has been 100% conclusive.

  2. What's Bigfoots diet?

    3. Since Bigfoot is believed to be a large mammal, many think he would follow an omnivorous diet consisting of eating mostly fish, elk, deer, rodents, plants, and fruits..

  3. How tall is Bigfoot?

    He's been described to be 6-19 feet tall.

  4. Are there more than one Bigfoot?

    5. Yes, there are believed to be at least 2,000 Sasquatches in the world. Consisting of both young and old Bigfoots. Families of them potentially roam the forests.

  5. What do scientists say about Bigfoot?

    6. Most scientists are skeptical of His existence due to the lack of evidence. No bones, hair samples, or DNA have proved him to be real. There are also no clear photos or videos that can identify Bigfoots actuality. Many researchers argue that if a population of large primates existed, we would have found more concrete proof by now.

  6. Has the FBI investigated Bigfoot?

    7. Yes. The FBI conducted a DNA test on what was initially believed to be Bigfoots hair, but was found to be deer hair.

  7. What would happen if Bigfoot was proven real?

    8. If Bigfoot were proven to exist with undeniable evidence of fossils, a body, or clear camera footage, this would open the door to new possibilities. Evidence of Bigfoots existence would transition his place in folklore to scientific fact. Tourism might increase in places associated with Bigfoot along with merchandise sales and more people would search for Bigfoot. If people saw that he was real, they would be more willing to believe in other cryptids and previously unimaginable possibilities.

  8. How has He impacted our Culture?

    9. Bigfoot has impacted our culture in all ways you can imagine. Inspiring famous films such as Harry and the Hendersons, along with many horror movies, documentaries centered around finding Bigfoot, and the children's movie Bigfoot Family on netflix. There are many other films and shows about, but this is just to list a few.

    Bigfoot has been so influential to our lives and has grown to be part of our culture. He's featured in advertisements, on merchandise, a mascot for the Portland Trail Blazers, and is an icon representing forests & national parks. Further, there have been many books and novels written about Bigfoot and his mysterious nature such as The Secret History of Bigfoot, written by John O'Connor. To further add on to his impressive resume, he has museums created in his name. He is one of the most recognizable beings of our century!
